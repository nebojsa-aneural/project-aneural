Our mission

At Aneural, our goal is to shift the current treatment paradigm that is based on the predefined stimulation, thus it is not personalized, which we will be exploiting as a competitive advantage. There is a dire need to move away from the treatments of an average patient and make the delivery of the stimulated therapy personalized, more precise, faster and more robust, this directly means a better Quality of Life for Patients.
